About

Posted on Jan 1, 0001

I am a sound artist, creative technologist, and educator working with sound using technology. I co-founded Black Shuck Co-operative, which produces moving image, audio and digital projects. I also work with young people in various settings, producing artworks and delivering training. I am completing an MRes Creative Computing course at the University of the Arts London (2022-2023).

Work with me!

With over 10 years of experience working in art, technology and education, I’ve done a bit of everything and am open to new opportunities to collaborate. Please email me for a CV or Portfolio tommie@blackshuck.co

Interests and skills:

  • Transdisciplinary Research (Artistic/Critical/Theory)
  • Youth-led artistic practice and collaboration
  • Machine Learning (audio specialism)
  • Programming (Python & Javascript mostly, but open to new challenges!)
  • Sound Design & Mixing for film, art installations & games
  • Interaction Design
  • DJing and hosting events as Odd Spiral
  • Synthesisers! Making and playing them!
