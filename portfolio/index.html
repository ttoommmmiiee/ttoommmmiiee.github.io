Water Drip Trig

These are a few experiments I did exploring the rhythmic qualities of dripping taps. I was inspired by the Water Drip Drum made by Ellen Fullman circa 1985. Her design involved a bucket filled with water with several different taps through which the water can drain. Depending on the tightness of the taps, the water drips onto other metal buckets below, causing a resonant percussive sound. The dripping tap is a classic example of a chaotic system.…

‘Womb of things to be and tomb of things that were’ is a film by Charles Pryor and Marleen Boschen that explores collaborative survival, containment and vulnerability through seeds.
