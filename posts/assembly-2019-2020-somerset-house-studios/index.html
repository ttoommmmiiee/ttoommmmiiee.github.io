Assembly - Somerset House Studios

Posted on Aug 1, 2020

In 2019, I collaborated with musicians and artists Beatrice Dillon, Haroon Mirza and, John Butcher & Lasse Marhaug for Somerset House Studios’ annual ASSEMBLY festival. I developed custom musical tools for each artist using Max/MSP, Wekinator and Touch OSC. The tools enabled the musicians to interface with sound and data from outside the performance venue and work with a large multichannel surround sound setup. https://www.somersethouse.org.uk/whats-on/assembly-christian-marclay

For ASSEMBLY 2020, I worked with Somerset House Studio to programme a bespoke online platform for presenting 360 spatialised audio in an interactive and immersive format. I presented this project at SOUNDSTACK conference for spatial audio at Queen Mary University London. I programmed the site using Omnitone, a JavaScript implementation of an ambisonic decoder that allows you to binaurally render an ambisonic recording directly on the browser, and three.js for 3d rendering in the browser. https://www.assembly2020.co/
